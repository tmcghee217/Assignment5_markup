Family Life

My life currently revolves around my daughter. Grace was born premature, and was in the NICU for 12 days; however, she is now two years old and is an active, happy toddler. We keep a fairly busy schedule with preschool, music class, activities at church, and frequent play dates or trips to the library.

Grace attends Early Learning two days a week, and this semester we signed up for Bongos class at Champaign School of Music. Earlier this year she was in dance, and next spring we're considering joining Soccer Planet. Thankfully, in Champaign we have many opportunities to exercise her mind, body, and spirit.

My husband and I also have two small dogs; both are rescues. Buster came from a rescue in Missouri. He is now nearly 15 years old and is showing his age. We adopted Ruthie from the Champaign County Humane Society1 7 years ago, and she is still a fun and loving addition to the family.

Personal Interests

In my free time, I like to do paper crafting which is "the collection of art forms employing paper or card as the primary artistic medium for the creation of three-dimensional objects" (wikipedia.com). Mostly I enjoy making cards that I never send and thinking how nice it would be to make some scrapbooks to pass down through the generations.

I also like to cook and bake, although I don't take much time to do it these days. I do sometimes read about cooking though, like this selection from Green Eggs and Ham:
